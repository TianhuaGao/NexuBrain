# Responsible Use Statement

NexuBrain was established by Dr. Tianhua Gao as a human-agent collaboration framework for researchers, developers, engineers, students, and knowledge workers.

The project is intended to help people build safer, more traceable, and more reusable second-brain systems for research, engineering, technical learning, source reading, experiments, writing, and long-term knowledge management.

## Intended Use

NexuBrain is intended for:

- research and development workflows;
- engineering project memory;
- academic and technical writing support;
- source reading and experiment traceability;
- human-agent collaboration;
- responsible use of external skills;
- evidence-aware knowledge management.

## Prohibited And Discouraged Use

Do not use NexuBrain to plan, enable, automate, conceal, or amplify dangerous, illegal, abusive, or unethical behavior.

This includes, but is not limited to:

- weapon development, weapon deployment, or operational harm against people;
- cyber abuse, unauthorized access, malware, credential theft, or exploit automation;
- fraud, impersonation, phishing, spam, or social manipulation;
- privacy invasion, unauthorized surveillance, stalking, or doxxing;
- academic misconduct, fabricated data, fabricated citations, or false research claims;
- unsafe medical, legal, financial, or safety-critical advice presented as verified fact;
- evasion of law, policy, safety controls, audits, or accountability;
- destructive automation against files, systems, repositories, or third-party services.

## Human Responsibility

NexuBrain is a framework for organizing collaboration. It does not replace human judgment.

Users are responsible for:

- complying with applicable laws, institutional rules, platform policies, and research ethics;
- reviewing agent outputs before relying on them;
- protecting credentials, private data, and sensitive records;
- keeping evidence, assumptions, observations, and conclusions clearly separated;
- ensuring that any external skills or tools are used within safe and authorized boundaries.
