# Evidence Chain Index

Evidence chains connect claims, implementations, experiments, literature, logs, and decisions.

## State Convention

- Verified: confirmed by a primary source or reproducible experiment.
- Observed: actually observed, but interpretation may remain incomplete.
- Hypothesis: plausible assumption that has not yet been verified.
- Failed: an attempt failed, and the failure has future value.
- Superseded: an older conclusion has been replaced by newer evidence.
